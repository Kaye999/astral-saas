Not a Horoscope
Millionaires don't use astrology. Billionaires do.
— Attributed to J.P. Morgan  ·  1910

Evangeline Adams kept rooms at Carnegie Hall and read the charts of Morgan, Mary Pickford, and King Edward VII. In 1914 she was tried for fortune-telling in New York and acquitted, after casting the judge's own chart from the witness stand. This is the tradition Synastra inherits: quiet, precise, and paid for.
