“Millionaires don't use astrology. Billionaires do.”
— Attributed to J.P. Morgan · Financier, Astrologer's Client, 1910

Evangeline Adams counted among her clientele Morgan, Mary Pickford, and King Edward VII. She was tried in New York in 1914 for fortune-telling; she cast the judge's chart from the witness stand and was acquitted. Her library, preserved at the Astrological Register, contains notes on the timing of the 1907 panic — written before it occurred.
